Hi, I'm Kareen Ghann, a freelance makeup artist and an enthusiast of Ghanaian cuisine. Originally from Ghana, I moved away just before my second birthday but have always held a deep love for my homeland's culinary delights. This passion for Ghanaian food is what inspired me to create this recipe page, where I share my favorite dishes and celebrate the flavors of Ghana.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills by participating in a bootcamp with Black Girls in Tech, balancing this exciting journey with the joys of motherhood. My son, Jude, who is two years old, is a significant part of my life.

Through this page, I hope to connect with fellow food enthusiasts and share the warmth and richness of Ghanaian cuisine. Whether you're a seasoned cook or new to these flavors, I invite you to explore these recipes and enjoy the taste of Ghana with me!
