Nkate Nkwan

Ghanaian peanut soup, also known as Nkate Nkwan, is a rich and hearty dish featuring a creamy base of groundnuts, often accompanied by tender meat and served with a side of rice balls or fufu.

Bofrot

Bofrot, a popular Ghanaian street snack, are sweet, fluffy, deep-fried doughnuts with a golden-brown crust, often enjoyed as a treat with a cup of milky tea.

Banku and Tilapia

Banku and Tilapia is a quintessential Ghanaian meal where slightly sour fermented corn dough is skillfully paired with grilled or fried tilapia, garnished with spicy pepper sauce and slices of fresh onion.
