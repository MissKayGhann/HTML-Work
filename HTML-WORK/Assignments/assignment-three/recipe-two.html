Ghanaian Bofrot (Puff-Puff)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (approx. 110°F/45°C)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Confectioners' sugar for dusting (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water to the dry ingredients and mix until the batter is smooth and sticky.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and allow the batter to rise in a warm place for about 1 - 1 ½ hours or until it doubles in size.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, turning once.
  6. Remove the bofrot with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  7. If desired, dust with confectioners' sugar before serving.
  8. Enjoy your bofrot warm and fresh.
